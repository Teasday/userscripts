Reader settings

Keyboard shortcuts

(^ = shift key)

  • f Toggle between fit display to container and width
  • ^f Toggle between fit display to height and no resize
  • g Toggle between the page rendering options
  • h Toggle between the reader directions
  • r Toggle header visibility
  • ^r Toggle header and sidebar visibility
  • up, w / down, s Scroll the screen up and down
  • left, a / right, d Turn page to left or right in single and double page modes, or go to the next or previous chapter in long strip mode
  • ^left, ^a / ^right, ^d Move only a single page in double page mode
